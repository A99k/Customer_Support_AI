# TechMart Electronics — Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty
All TechMart products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions under normal use.

## What Is Covered
- Manufacturing defects
- Component failure under normal use
- Battery defects (first 6 months only, batteries are considered consumables afterward)

## What Is Not Covered
- Accidental damage (drops, spills, cracked screens)
- Water damage not caused by a manufacturing defect
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Normal wear and tear (e.g., cosmetic scratches)

## Extended Warranty
Customers can purchase TechMart Protect at checkout for an additional 1 or 2 years of coverage, which also includes one accidental damage claim per year with a $49 service fee.

## How to File a Warranty Claim
1. Go to "My Orders," select the product, and click "File Warranty Claim."
2. Describe the issue and attach photos or videos if applicable.
3. Our technical team reviews the claim within 2 business days.
4. Approved claims result in a free repair, replacement, or store credit, at TechMart's discretion.

## Warranty Transferability
The standard 1-year warranty is tied to the original purchase and is not transferable if the product is resold.
