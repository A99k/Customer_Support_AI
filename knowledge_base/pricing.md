# TechMart Electronics — Pricing & Plans

## Product Pricing
Product prices are listed on each product page and include applicable taxes for domestic orders. Prices may vary during promotional events (Flash Sales, Holiday Sales).

## Price Match Guarantee
If you find an identical product at a lower price from an authorized retailer within 7 days of purchase, TechMart will refund the difference. Marketplace sellers and clearance items are excluded.

## TechMart Premium Subscription
- Monthly Plan: $9.99/month — free express shipping, early access to sales, extended return window (45 days).
- Annual Plan: $89.99/year (equivalent to $7.49/month) — same benefits as monthly, billed once a year.
- Premium can be canceled anytime from Account Settings > Subscriptions.

## Bulk & Business Pricing
Businesses purchasing 10+ units of the same product qualify for tiered discounts. Contact business-sales@techmart.example for a custom quote.

## Financing (EMI)
Orders above $200 are eligible for 3, 6, or 12-month EMI plans through our partnered payment providers, subject to credit approval. Interest rates vary by provider and tenure.
