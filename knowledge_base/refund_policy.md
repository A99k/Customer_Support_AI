# TechMart Electronics — Refund & Return Policy

## Return Window
Most products can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the item is unused, in its original packaging, and includes all accessories and manuals.

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned unless defective: opened software licenses, gift cards, and personal care electronics (e.g., earbuds, shavers) once the hygiene seal is broken.

## Refund Timeline
Once a returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse (typically 2–3 business days), refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. Bank processing times may add a few additional days.

## Defective or Damaged Items
If a product arrives defective or damaged, customers can request a free replacement or a full refund, including original shipping costs, within 14 days of delivery. Photos of the damage may be requested to expedite processing.

## Restocking Fee
A 10% restocking fee applies to large appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, TVs above 55 inches) returned for reasons other than defects.

## How to Initiate a Refund
1. Go to "My Orders" and select the item you wish to return.
2. Choose a reason for the return.
3. Print the prepaid return label (for defective items) or arrange pickup.
4. Once the item is received, the refund is processed automatically.

## Subscription & Service Refunds
Subscription services (e.g., TechMart Premium) can be canceled anytime. Refunds for the current billing period are only issued if canceled within 48 hours of the charge. Refunds are not issued for partially used billing periods after that window.
