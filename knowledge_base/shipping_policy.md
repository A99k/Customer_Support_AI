# TechMart Electronics — Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options
- Standard Shipping: 4–6 business days, free on orders above $50 ($5.99 otherwise).
- Express Shipping: 1–2 business days, $14.99 flat rate.
- Same-Day Delivery: Available in select metro areas for orders placed before 12 PM, $19.99 flat rate.

## Order Processing Time
Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

## International Shipping
TechMart ships to over 40 countries. International orders typically take 7–14 business days and may be subject to customs duties, which are the responsibility of the customer.

## Tracking
A tracking number is emailed once the order ships. Tracking can also be viewed under "My Orders" in your account.

## Delayed or Lost Packages
If a package shows no tracking movement for more than 5 business days (domestic) or 10 business days (international), contact support to open an investigation with the carrier. Confirmed lost packages are reshipped or refunded at no cost.

## Delivery Address Issues
TechMart is not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer at checkout. Please double-check your address before placing an order.
