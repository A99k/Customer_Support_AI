# TechMart Electronics — Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Orders
Q: How do I create a TechMart account?
A: Click "Sign Up" on the top right of the website, enter your email and password, and verify your email address via the link we send you.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard. Each order shows a live status: Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, or Delivered, along with a tracking number.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
A: You can change the shipping address only if the order status is still "Processing." Once an order has shipped, the address cannot be changed.

## Payments
Q: What payment methods does TechMart accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and TechMart Gift Cards. EMI options are available on orders above $200.

Q: Why was my payment declined?
A: Payments are typically declined due to insufficient funds, an expired card, or a mismatch between the billing address and the address on file with your bank. Contact your bank or try an alternate payment method.

## Products
Q: Do TechMart products come with a warranty?
A: Yes. Most electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranties (2 or 3 years) can be purchased at checkout.

Q: How do I know if a product is in stock?
A: Product availability is shown in real time on each product page. "In Stock," "Low Stock," and "Out of Stock" labels are updated continuously.

## Support
Q: What are TechMart's customer support hours?
A: Our support team is available Monday–Saturday, 9 AM–9 PM (local time). Live chat is available 24/7 for urgent billing and account issues.

Q: How do I contact a human agent?
A: If our AI assistant cannot resolve your issue, type "talk to a human" at any time and your conversation will be escalated to a support representative.
